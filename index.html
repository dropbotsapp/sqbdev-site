SQB™ – Structured Query Bot

SQB™ is a scoped-logic framework that wraps AI systems with predictable, controllable, and consistent flows. It is currently in use across DropBots, Clarity Engine, and other deterministic AI-layered systems.

What Makes It Different?

Unlike general-purpose bots or freeform agents, SQBs are designed for predictable, scoped interactions — with enforceable logic boundaries and transparent interaction layers.

Applications

- Client-facing support and intake
- Structured legal, real estate, or medical workflows
- Embedded logic bots for specific business units
